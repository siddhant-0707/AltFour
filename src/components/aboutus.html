HOW IT CAN HELP


NFT marketplaces provide a platform for creators to showcase their work and monetise their talent. Digital Artists in India not only have a platform to advertise their work on AltFour but are also connected to buyers specific to their need. Moreover, awareness and recognition of this art form along with the artists is also generated through AltFour. It is a respectable space for this underrated art community.

Any marketplace provides an opportunity for creators to earn a significant amount of money from their creative works. With the continuous growth of the demand for NFTs, prices of these unique digital assets can rise. This along with the unprecedented royalty shares provided by AltFour allow creators to earn substantial sums of money.

NFT marketplaces offer an excellent opportunity for people in India to learn about blockchain technology, digital art, and cryptocurrency. They can also learn about smart contracts and other aspects of the NFT market, which can help them in their future careers.

NFT marketplaces provide a platform for collectors to acquire unique digital collectibles. Collecting NFTs can be a fun and rewarding hobby for people, especially youngsters, in India.
